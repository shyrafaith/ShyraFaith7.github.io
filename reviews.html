Game Review

Top 3 Best PC Games

  • Final Fantasy XIV

    • Final Fantasy XIV, developed and published by Square Enix, is an extraordinary MMORPG that invites players into a breathtaking realm filled with grand adventures, captivating stories, and a vibrant community. Its engrossing narrative weaves a compelling tale across a multitude of expansions, each offering its own unique storyline, memorable characters, and awe-inspiring locations. The world of Eorzea is a visual marvel, beautifully realized with attention to detail and artistic flair. The combat mechanics strike a delicate balance between accessibility and depth, allowing both casual and hardcore players to find their place. The flexible class system, known as Jobs, enables players to switch between roles and playstyles on the same character, providing versatility and encouraging experimentation. Beyond combat, the game offers a wide array of activities and content to suit every player's preferences. Final Fantasy XIV is an MMORPG that offers an unforgettable journey into a realm of magic, friendship, and endless possibilities. It encourages cooperation and collaboration, with a robust guild system and player-driven events. The development team's commitment to the game's ongoing improvement and expansion is commendable, with regular updates introducing new content, quality-of-life improvements, and exciting features. Additionally, the community-driven events and seasonal celebrations bring a sense of festivity and unity to the player base. Final Fantasy XIV is a triumph in the MMORPG genre, offering an unforgettable journey into a realm of magic, friendship, and endless possibilities.




  • Street Fighter II

    • Street Fighter II, launched in 1991, is a legendary and important fighting game. It includes a wide cast of memorable characters, gameplay mechanisms that are both accessible and deep, timeless visual and aural design, and a number of game modes to keep players involved. It transformed the competitive gaming environment by popularizing structured tournaments and professional gaming and motivating future generations of players. Its influence extended beyond its initial success, as it transformed the competitive gaming environment and popularized the notion of structured tournaments and professional gaming. Its effect can be seen in innumerable subsequent combat games, influencing the genre and inspiring generations of gamers.




  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

    • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, developed and published by Nintendo, redefines the open-world genre with its boundless exploration, immersive gameplay, and captivating world. The game's stunning visual design, blending cel-shaded art with breathtaking landscapes, creates a mesmerizing and immersive environment. It emphasizes freedom and exploration, providing players with an immense open world and encouraging them to forge their own path and discover secrets at their own pace. The gameplay mechanics of Breath of the Wild are a masterclass in seamless design, with physics-based interactions, dynamic weather system, and inventive puzzles creating a world that feels alive and responsive. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a masterpiece of game design and a testament to the magic of the Zelda franchise. Its vast open world, seamless gameplay, stunning visuals, and evocative storytelling combine to deliver an unparalleled gaming experience. Players can experiment and think outside the box, unlocking new possibilities for exploration and combat. The strategic combat system requires both skill and resourcefulness, and the storytelling is beautifully woven into its world. The game's attention to detail extends to its immersive sound design and enchanting musical score, creating an atmosphere that fully immerses players in the game's world. The game's vast open world, seamless gameplay, stunning visuals, and evocative storytelling combine to deliver an unparalleled gaming experience.




Top 3 Best XBOX Games



  • Red Dead Redemption 2

    • Red Dead Redemption 2 is a monumental achievement in video game storytelling, offering a meticulously crafted world that immerses players in a captivating Wild West adventure. Set in 1899, players assume the role of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang, as they navigate the waning days of the Old West. The game's attention to detail is staggering, evident in the meticulously designed landscapes, bustling towns, and the nuanced interactions between characters and their surroundings. The vastness of the open world encourages exploration, rewarding players with hidden treasures, unique encounters, and breathtaking vistas. The gameplay mechanics are refined and offer a balance between action-packed gunfights, intense horseback chases, and slower-paced moments of reflection. The controls feel intuitive, and the variety of missions and side activities ensures that players never run out of things to do. Red Dead Redemption 2 is an undisputed masterpiece that raises the bar for storytelling and world-building in video games. It blends narrative-driven missions with open-ended exploration, and boasts an exceptional multiplayer mode, Red Dead Online, which expands on the game's world and lets players embark on their own adventures with friends. Graphically, the game is a visual marvel, with photorealistic character models, stunningly rendered landscapes, lighting and weather effects, and a hauntingly beautiful score and superb voice acting. However, the game's pacing can feel deliberate and methodical at times, but this deliberate pace contributes to the overall atmosphere and helps build a deeper connection to the characters and their struggles. Red Dead Redemption 2 is an essential experience that shouldn't be missed.




  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

    • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, developed by CD Projekt Red, is a shining example of what a role-playing game can achieve. It has a vast and immersive open world, gripping storyline, and memorable characters, making it one of the greatest games of all time. Players step into the shoes of Geralt of Rivia, a renowned monster hunter known as a Witcher, and are transported into a sprawling and meticulously crafted world. The game's narrative is captivating, weaving together political intrigue, personal quests, and the search for Geralt's adopted daughter, Ciri. The attention to detail in The Witcher 3 is unparalleled, with diverse landscapes, dynamic weather, day-night cycles, and bustling towns inhabited by a rich tapestry of characters. The gameplay mechanics in The Witcher 3 strike a perfect balance between accessibility and depth, with Geralt's combat skills and magical abilities making for exhilarating battles against a wide variety of enemies. The voice acting is top-notch, bringing each character to life with nuanced performances that draw players deeper into the world of The Witch The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a masterpiece of storytelling and game design, featuring stunning visuals, realistic facial animations, breathtaking vistas, and a hauntingly beautiful soundtrack. However, the game's sheer size and depth can be overwhelming for some players. However, for those willing to invest the time and immerse themselves in this epic journey, The Witcher 3 rewards with an unforgettable experience that will leave a lasting impression. Whether you're a fan of the fantasy genre or simply appreciate exceptional game craftsmanship, The Witcher 3 is an absolute must-play and a shining gem in the realm of video games.




  • Dishonored 2

    • Dishonored 2, developed by Arkane Studios, is a remarkable stealth-action game that offers a richly realized world, diverse gameplay options, and a compelling narrative. Players step into the shoes of the supernatural assassin Corvo Attano or his daughter, Empress Emily Kaldwin, and the story unfolds with a blend of political intrigue, revenge, and supernatural powers. The gameplay in Dishonored 2 excels in providing players with an array of options for approaching each mission. The level design is superb, featuring intricate and sprawling environments filled with hidden paths, secrets, and multiple routes to reach objectives. One of the standout features of Dishonored 2 is its attention to detail and world-building. Karnaca is a beautifully realized city, with its decaying architecture, vibrant markets, and atmospheric districts. Dishonored 2 is a stealth-action game that successfully builds upon the strengths of its predecessor. It is visually stunning, with its distinctive art style and attention to detail. The environments are richly detailed, and the character models are beautifully rendered. However, the AI can occasionally be inconsistent and the pacing uneven. Despite these minor flaws, Dishonored 2 remains an exceptional gaming experience. Its compelling narrative, flexible gameplay mechanics, and immersive world-building combine to create a game that encourages experimentation and rewards player agency. Fans of the stealth genre and those seeking a game that values player choice will find much to enjoy in Dishonored 2, making it a standout title in the gaming landscape.




Top 3 Best PlayStation Games

  • God of War

    • God of War, the 2018 reboot of the legendary franchise, is a monumental achievement in the world of gaming. It follows Kratos, the Spartan warrior turned god, as he embarks on a deeply personal journey with his son, Atreus. The story is beautifully crafted, delving into themes of redemption, parenthood, and the consequences of one's past actions. The gameplay mechanics in God of War are nothing short of extraordinary. The combat system has been completely overhauled from its predecessors, adopting a more deliberate and immersive style. The addition of Atreus as a companion adds a strategic element to battles, and the seamless integration of puzzle-solving elements and exploration into the gameplay ensures a well-rounded experience. Visually, God of War is a masterpiece. God of War is a game that features breathtakingly detailed environments inspired by Norse mythology. It features character designs that are meticulously crafted and filled with stunning vistas. The game's graphical fidelity, coupled with the seamless transition between gameplay and cutscenes, creates a seamless and immersive experience. Bear McCreary's musical score captures the grandeur and emotional depth of the game. God of War is a true showcase of the PlayStation 4's capabilities and a testament to the artistry of game development. It transcends the boundaries of the medium, delivering a compelling narrative, immersive gameplay, and breathtaking visuals. Santa Monica Studio has raised the bar for what can be achieved in the realm of video games, and God of War is a testament to the power of this incredible art form.




  • Horizon Zero Dawn

    • Horizon Zero Dawn is an open-world action RPG developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where nature has reclaimed the land and mechanical beasts roam the earth. Players step into the shoes of Aloy, a skilled hunter and outcast seeking to uncover the mysteries of her world. The rich and detailed world of Horizon Zero Dawn is a visual feast, with stunning vistas, lush forests, and desolate ruins that create an immersive and believable environment. The narrative of Horizon Zero Dawn is a masterclass in storytelling, filled with intriguing characters, complex relationships, and thought-provoking themes that explore the nature of humanity and the consequences of its actions. The gameplay mechanics in Horizon Zero Dawn are nothing short of exceptional. Horizon Zero Dawn is an action RPG that features Aloy's agile movement and combat prowess. The game's robust skill tree allows for customization and specialization, and offers a wealth of side quests, collectibles, and activities. The attention to detail in the game is commendable, and the day-night cycle and dynamic weather systems add depth and realism to the world. The game boasts stunning visuals, with impressive attention to detail and breathtakingly realistic character models. Guerrilla Games has created a true masterpiece that sets a new standard for open-world gaming. Whether you are a fan of action RPGs or simply enjoy exploring richly realized worlds, Horizon Zero Dawn is an absolute must-play that will leave you yearning for more.




  • Uncharted: The Lost Legacy

    • Uncharted: The Lost Legacy is a standalone expansion to the beloved Uncharted series, developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It takes players on a thrilling journey through exotic locales, showcasing the franchise's trademark blend of cinematic storytelling, dynamic gameplay, and breathtaking visuals. One of the standout features of the game is its strong female protagonists, Chloe Frazer and Nadine Ross. The narrative revolves around the search for an ancient artifact in the war-torn Western Ghats of India. The game's writing and voice acting shine through, as the characters' banter and interactions create a sense of camaraderie and charm. The gameplay mechanics in The Lost Legacy build upon the solid foundation established in the main Uncharted games. Players engage in thrilling gunfights, solve environmental puzzles, and navigate treacherous terrain with fluidity and precision. Uncharted: The Lost Legacy is a worthy addition to the Uncharted series, offering a thrilling and immersive adventure with memorable characters, spectacular visuals, and engaging gameplay. It features a lock-picking mechanic and the use of the protagonist's smartphone, and Naughty Dog's attention to detail in the lush landscapes, intricate ruins, and vibrant environments. The game's graphical fidelity is impressive, with breathtaking vistas and realistic character models. The soundtrack captures the spirit of adventure and heightens the sense of danger, creating an engaging atmosphere throughout the game. While it may not reach the same heights as its predecessors in terms of narrative depth, it still offers an enjoyable and polished experience that fans of the series will appreciate.

Ultimate strike a perfect balance between accessibility and depth, offering a variety of gameplay modes, from fun-filled multiplayer brawls with friends to intense one-on-one battles. The controls are intuitive and responsive, allowing players to execute a multitude of attacks, special moves, and combos effortlessly. The dynamic stages add an extra layer of excitement and strategy to the matches. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate truly shines when it comes to its multiplayer experience, offering an endless amount of fun and replayability. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a must-have title for Nintendo Switch owners and a love letter to gaming enthusiasts everywhere. It features multiplayer modes, customizable rulesets, and an online matchmaking system. The game's visual presentation is stunning, featuring vibrant colors and intricate details. The game runs smoothly and features an impressive soundtrack that pays homage to the vast library of gaming history. The game also includes spirits and challenges, providing additional content and goals to keep players engaged. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is an unrivaled crossover experience that brings people together and ensures countless hours of exhilarating battles and unforgettable gaming moments.

Players are whisked away to a deserted island and given the freedom to shape and customize every aspect of their new home. The charming anthropomorphic animal villagers add life and personality to the experience, forging friendships and engaging in lighthearted conversations. The game's relaxed and immersive gameplay, combined with the game's charming aesthetics and gentle soundtrack, creates a tranquil and soothing atmosphere that provides a much-needed escape from the stresses of everyday life. The customization options in Animal Crossing: New Horizons are vast and impressive. Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a game that allows players to express their creativity and personalize their island. It encourages social interaction and collaboration, allowing players to visit each other's islands, trade items, and engage in activities together. The game's vibrant and charming art style, coupled with the attention to detail in the environmental design, creates a visually appealing and immersive world. The game's ability to create a sense of community and provide a serene virtual getaway has made it a cultural phenomenon that continues to captivate players of all ages. It reminds us of the simple joys of life and the importance of finding solace in the little things.




  • Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

    • Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, developed and published by Nintendo, is a definitive and exhilarating racing experience that brings the beloved Mario Kart franchise to new heights. Released for the Nintendo Switch in 2017, it combines the best elements of the original Wii U version with a host of additional content, making it the ultimate package for both new and returning players. The game offers a wide selection of colorful and iconic characters from the Mario universe, each with their own unique attributes and vehicles. The gameplay mechanics in Mario Kart 8 Deluxe strike a perfect balance between accessibility and depth. The game offers multiple control options, including traditional controls, motion controls, and support for the Nintendo Switch's Joy-Con controllers. Additionally, the game supports both local and online multiplayer, allowing players to compete against friends or challenge opponents from around the world. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is a must-have title for Nintendo Switch owners and a standout entry in the long-running franchise. It provides hours of fun and excitement for players of all ages, with its fast-paced races, intuitive controls, stunning visuals, and robust multiplayer options. The game's visual presentation is a treat for the eyes, running at a smooth 60 frames per second. The soundtrack is a delightful mix of catchy tunes and nostalgic melodies that complement the action on the tracks. The game is a shining example of what makes the series so beloved and cements its status as one of the best kart racing games ever made.
